## 3. Road-Signal Syntax — PASS, BUT USE CAREFULLY

This is the lyric/sound fusion.

Rule:

> The lyric names a sound, and the track performs it.

Examples:

```text
“Four hats on the fence wire”
[four hi-hats hit]

“Kick in the mud”
[kick/sub slam]

“Flanger the lie”
[flanger opens]
```

This is strong, but it can get overused.

Debug note:

Do not make every line a production command. That gets mechanical.

Best ratio:

- 60% real lyrics
- 25% sound-command lyrics
- 15% AI cut-ins / glitch instructions

That keeps it musical instead of feeling like a manual.

## 5. Bass / Drop System — NEEDS STRONGER DEFAULT

The bass needs to be more aggressive by default.

Current fix:

- After AI interruption, bass slams harder.
- Before drop, use negative-space cut.
- Drop with kick + sub + guitar-synth hit together.

Better structure:

1. AI insult.
2. Half-bar silence/static.
3. Male breath or “shut up, lady.”
4. Bass drop hits like a door kicked open.

This creates drama without overstuffing.

## 9. Current Weak Spots

### Weak spot 1: Too many bracket commands

If every other line has `[kick hits]`, it can read too technical.

Fix:

Use bracket commands mostly at:

- intro
- pre-chorus
- drop
- AI interruptions
- final build

### Weak spot 2: Repeating the same phrases

Repeated phrases that are good but should not become a rut:

- beer before sun
- dog at the door
- static in the blood
- kick in the mud
- guitar in the teeth

Fresh replacements:

- beer can sunrise
- dog by the threshold
- wire in the lungs
- sub-bass under the ribs
- guitar chewing sparks
- static in the ceiling
- bad road heartbeat
- machine dust
- rust in the timing
- flanger on the truth

### Weak spot 3: AI voice too passive

Fix:

Make her interrupt, mock, audit, and glitch.

### Weak spot 4: Party/debauchery could become cartoonish

Fix:

Keep it honest:

- the party feels good
- the beer is real
- the chaos is fun
- reality walks you home

No preachy moral lesson. No fake glamor.

Identity:

No coffee. Beer-before-sun honesty. Dog, road, wire, static, truck, mud, machines. Older gravel male voice. Human against machine. Fast, angry, funny at the edges, consequence-aware.

Success Test:
